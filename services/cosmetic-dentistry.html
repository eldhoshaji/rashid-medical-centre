Porcelain and Composite Veneers

Dental veneers are thin, custom-made shells that are bonded to the front surface of your teeth to improve their shape, colour, size, or alignment. They are one of the most versatile and effective cosmetic dental solutions available, and we offer both porcelain and composite options at our dental clinic in Ajman.

Porcelain Veneers

Porcelain veneers are crafted from high-quality dental ceramic that closely mimics the translucency and lustre of natural tooth enamel. They are highly resistant to staining and can last 15 years or longer with proper care. Porcelain veneers are ideal for patients who want a dramatic yet natural-looking smile transformation and are the foundation of most Hollywood smile treatments.

Composite Veneers

Composite veneers are sculpted directly onto the teeth using tooth-coloured resin material. They require less enamel removal than porcelain veneers and can often be completed in a single visit. Composite veneers are an excellent choice for patients looking for a more affordable cosmetic solution or those who want to correct minor imperfections such as small chips, gaps, or uneven edges.

Professional Teeth Whitening

Over time, teeth can become stained or discoloured due to coffee, tea, red wine, tobacco, and the natural ageing process. Over-the-counter whitening products often deliver inconsistent results and can cause sensitivity when used incorrectly. Professional in-office teeth whitening at Rashid Medical Centre offers a safe, fast, and dramatically more effective alternative.

Our whitening treatments use clinically proven, high-concentration whitening agents that are carefully applied under controlled conditions. In just one session lasting approximately 45 to 60 minutes, your teeth can be lightened by several shades. Dr. Manoj Mathew ensures that your gums and soft tissues are fully protected throughout the procedure, minimising any risk of sensitivity. We also provide custom take-home whitening kits for patients who prefer to brighten their smile gradually in the comfort of their own home.

Dental Bonding

Dental bonding is a quick, minimally invasive cosmetic procedure in which tooth-coloured composite resin is applied to the teeth to repair chips, cracks, gaps, or discolouration. It is one of the most affordable cosmetic treatments and can be completed in a single appointment without the need for anaesthesia in most cases.

Bonding is particularly well suited for patients who want to improve the appearance of one or two teeth without committing to a full set of veneers. The results are immediate, and with proper care, bonded restorations can last several years before needing a touch-up. At our dental clinic in Ajman, we use premium composite materials that blend seamlessly with your natural teeth for an invisible repair.

Smile Design

Smile design is a comprehensive planning process that takes into account the shape of your face, the proportion of your lips, your gum line, and the alignment of your teeth to create a smile that is uniquely yours. Rather than applying a one-size-fits-all approach, Dr. Manoj Mathew uses digital imaging and careful measurements to map out the ideal dimensions for each tooth.

This meticulous approach ensures that the final result looks balanced and harmonious from every angle. Smile design may incorporate a combination of veneers, whitening, bonding, and even orthodontic treatments depending on the patient's starting point and desired outcome. It is the blueprint behind every successful cosmetic transformation at Rashid Medical Centre.
