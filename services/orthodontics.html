Types of Braces We Offer

Choosing the right type of braces is one of the most important decisions in your orthodontic journey. As a leading dental clinic in Ajman, we provide four proven systems so that every patient can find a solution that fits their lifestyle, budget, and treatment goals.

⚙

Metal Braces

The most time-tested and cost-effective orthodontic system. Modern metal brackets are smaller and more comfortable than ever before. Ideal for complex cases and suitable for patients of all ages, metal braces deliver reliable, predictable results.

◆

Ceramic Braces

Tooth-coloured ceramic brackets blend seamlessly with your natural enamel, offering a discreet alternative to metal braces. They work using the same mechanics but with significantly improved aesthetics, making them popular among working professionals and older teenagers.

✔

Damon Self-Ligating Braces

The Damon system uses a slide mechanism instead of elastic ties to connect archwires, reducing friction and the need for frequent adjustments. Patients often experience shorter treatment times, fewer appointments, and greater comfort throughout the process.

★

Clear Aligners

Removable, virtually invisible trays that gradually shift your teeth into position. Clear aligners are ideal for mild to moderate alignment issues and offer unmatched convenience. You can eat, brush, and floss without restriction throughout your treatment.

The Orthodontic Treatment Process

Understanding what to expect helps you feel comfortable before, during, and after treatment. At Rashid Medical Centre, we follow a structured approach to ensure the best possible results for every patient.

1

Initial Consultation & Assessment

Dr. Reeja Moideen performs a thorough clinical examination, takes digital X-rays, and evaluates your bite, jaw alignment, and facial symmetry. This comprehensive assessment forms the foundation of your personalised treatment plan.

2

Customised Treatment Plan

Based on the diagnostic records, Dr. Reeja will present you with a detailed treatment plan that outlines the recommended type of braces, the estimated duration of treatment, the expected outcome, and a transparent breakdown of costs. You will have the opportunity to ask questions and select the option that best suits your needs.

3

Braces Placement or Aligner Delivery

The placement of fixed braces typically takes 60 to 90 minutes and is completely painless. For clear aligner patients, you will receive your first set of custom-made trays along with detailed wearing instructions.

4

Regular Adjustments & Monitoring

You will visit the clinic every four to six weeks for adjustments to your braces or to receive your next set of aligners. During these appointments, Dr. Reeja monitors your progress, makes any necessary modifications, and ensures your treatment stays on schedule.

5

Braces Removal & Retention

Once your teeth have reached their ideal positions, the braces are removed and a retainer is provided to maintain your new smile. Dr. Reeja will advise on retainer wear and schedule follow-up visits to ensure long-term stability.

Conditions We Treat with Orthodontics

Orthodontic treatment at our dental clinic in Ajman addresses a wide range of alignment and bite issues, including:

  • Crowded teeth — teeth that overlap or twist due to insufficient space in the jaw.
  • Spacing and gaps — noticeable spaces between teeth that can affect appearance and oral hygiene.
  • Overbite — upper front teeth that protrude significantly over the lower teeth.
  • Underbite — lower teeth that sit in front of the upper teeth when biting.
  • Crossbite — upper teeth that bite inside the lower teeth on one or both sides.
  • Open bite — front teeth that do not touch when the back teeth are closed.
  • Midline discrepancy — the centre of the upper and lower teeth does not align.

Left untreated, these conditions can lead to uneven tooth wear, jaw pain, difficulty chewing, and increased risk of cavities and gum disease. Early intervention with orthodontic treatment can prevent these complications and improve overall oral health.

Frequently Asked Questions About Orthodontics in Ajman

How much do braces cost in Ajman?

How long does orthodontic treatment take?
Orthodontic treatment duration depends on the severity of misalignment and the type of braces used. Most cases are completed within 12 to 24 months. Minor alignment issues may be resolved in as little as 6 months with clear aligners, while more complex cases involving jaw discrepancies may take up to 30 months. Dr. Reeja Moideen will provide a personalised timeline during your initial consultation.
What is the best age to get braces?
The ideal age for an initial orthodontic evaluation is around 7 years old, when the first permanent molars and incisors have erupted. However, orthodontic treatment is effective at any age. Many adults in Ajman are now choosing braces or clear aligners to improve their smile. At Rashid Medical Centre, we treat children, teenagers, and adults with equal expertise.
Are ceramic braces better than metal braces?
Ceramic braces and metal braces are both highly effective for teeth alignment. Ceramic braces use tooth-coloured brackets that blend with your natural teeth, making them a more discreet option. Metal braces are generally more durable and slightly more affordable. The best choice depends on your lifestyle, budget, and aesthetic preferences. Dr. Reeja Moideen at Rashid Medical Centre can help you decide which option suits you best.
