Comprehensive Primary Healthcare in Ajman

At Rashid Medical Centre, our general medicine department provides comprehensive primary healthcare services to individuals and families throughout Ajman and surrounding areas. As one of the most established medical centres in Ajman, we have been serving the community for over 25 years with a commitment to accessible, high-quality care that addresses your everyday health needs.

Our experienced general practitioners (GPs) are your first point of contact for all non-emergency health concerns. Whether you are experiencing symptoms of a common illness, need a routine health check-up, or require ongoing management of a chronic condition, our doctors in Ajman are here to help you with personalised medical attention and evidence-based treatment plans.

Our General Medicine Services

Routine Health Check-Ups and Preventive Screenings

Prevention is always better than cure. Our GP services in Ajman include thorough annual health check-ups designed to detect potential health issues before they become serious. These screenings cover blood pressure monitoring, blood sugar and cholesterol testing, BMI assessment, and age-appropriate health evaluations. Regular check-ups are especially important for adults over 40 and those with a family history of chronic diseases.

Chronic Disease Management

Living with a chronic condition requires consistent, knowledgeable medical support. Our doctors in Ajman specialise in the long-term management of conditions such as diabetes, hypertension (high blood pressure), high cholesterol, asthma, and thyroid disorders. We work closely with each patient to develop tailored treatment plans that include medication management, lifestyle modifications, and regular follow-up consultations to track progress and adjust care as needed.

Treatment of Common Infections and Illnesses

From seasonal flu and respiratory infections to urinary tract infections, skin conditions, and gastrointestinal issues, our general medicine team provides prompt diagnosis and effective treatment. We use modern diagnostic tools to ensure accurate identification of your condition and prescribe the most appropriate course of treatment to get you back on your feet quickly.

Vaccinations and Immunizations

Staying up to date with vaccinations is one of the most effective ways to protect yourself and your family. Our medical centre in Ajman offers a full range of vaccinations for children and adults, including seasonal flu shots, travel vaccinations, and routine immunizations as per UAE Ministry of Health guidelines. Our team will help you understand which vaccinations are recommended based on your age, health history, and travel plans.

Family Medicine for All Ages

Rashid Medical Centre is truly a family-friendly healthcare provider. Our GPs deliver care tailored to every stage of life:

  • Children: Growth monitoring, childhood vaccinations, treatment of common paediatric illnesses, and school health clearances
  • Adults: Preventive screenings, acute illness treatment, workplace medical certificates, and chronic disease management
  • Seniors: Geriatric health assessments, medication reviews, management of age-related conditions, and wellness counselling

Walk-In and Appointment Availability

We understand that health concerns do not always follow a schedule. That is why Rashid Medical Centre offers both walk-in consultations and pre-booked appointments to suit your needs. Our clinic is open Saturday through Thursday from 9:00 AM to 1:30 PM and 5:00 PM to 9:30 PM, with Sunday available by appointment only. Whether you need urgent attention for a sudden illness or a planned visit for a routine check-up, our team ensures minimal wait times and attentive care for every patient who walks through our doors.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to see a GP at Rashid Medical Centre?
No, Rashid Medical Centre welcomes both walk-in patients and scheduled appointments. While appointments help reduce wait times, you can visit our clinic during working hours (Saturday to Thursday, 9:00 AM - 1:30 PM & 5:00 PM - 9:30 PM) without prior booking. Simply walk in and our reception team will assist you promptly.
What insurance plans are accepted at your medical centre in Ajman?
Rashid Medical Centre accepts a wide range of insurance providers including Daman, Oman Insurance, ADNIC, NAS, Neuron, Sukoon, Mednet, and Nextcare. If you are unsure whether your plan is covered, call us at +971 6 744 4272 and our front desk team will verify your coverage before your visit.
What general medicine services do you offer?
Our general medicine department offers comprehensive primary healthcare including routine health check-ups, chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, cholesterol), treatment of common infections, vaccinations and immunizations, family medicine consultations for all ages, sick notes and medical certificates, and preventive health screenings.
Can your GP treat children and elderly patients?
Yes, our general practitioners provide family medicine services for patients of all ages, from children to elderly adults. We offer age-appropriate screenings, vaccinations, growth monitoring for children, and geriatric care for senior patients. Our doctors take a holistic approach to ensure every family member receives the right level of care.
