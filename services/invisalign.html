What Is Invisalign?

Invisalign is the world's leading clear aligner system for straightening teeth without the need for metal brackets or wires. Using a series of custom-made, virtually invisible removable trays, Invisalign gradually shifts your teeth into the correct position over time. Each set of aligners is precision-engineered using advanced 3D imaging technology, ensuring a comfortable fit and predictable results.

At Rashid Medical Centre, our dental clinic in Ajman, we offer Invisalign treatment for adults and teenagers who want a straighter smile without the appearance and inconvenience of traditional braces. Whether you are dealing with crowded teeth, gaps, an overbite, underbite, or crossbite, Invisalign may be the right solution for you.

How Invisalign Works

The Invisalign system uses a sequence of clear plastic aligners, each one slightly different from the last. You wear each set for one to two weeks, and with each change, your teeth move a fraction of a millimetre closer to their final, ideal position. The aligners are removable, which means you can take them out to eat, drink, brush, and floss normally.

Treatment begins with a detailed consultation and a digital 3D scan of your teeth. This scan is used to create a precise treatment plan that maps out every stage of your tooth movement from start to finish. You can even preview your expected result before treatment begins. Your custom aligners are then fabricated and delivered, and you visit the clinic periodically for progress check-ups.

Invisalign Treatment Timeline

Every patient's journey is unique, but here is what you can generally expect when you begin Invisalign treatment at Rashid Medical Centre:

1

Initial Consultation

Dr. Reeja examines your teeth, takes digital X-rays, and discusses your goals. She will assess whether Invisalign is the right option for your case and provide an honest estimate of treatment duration and cost.

2

3D Digital Scan and Treatment Plan

A precise 3D digital scan of your teeth is captured using our intraoral scanner. No messy putty impressions needed. A customised treatment plan is created, mapping each stage of tooth movement. You can preview your projected final smile.

3

Aligner Fabrication and Fitting

Your custom aligners are manufactured and shipped to the clinic. During a short appointment, Dr. Reeja fits your first set, ensures a comfortable fit, and provides instructions on wear and care. Small tooth-coloured attachments may be placed on select teeth to assist with specific movements.

4

Wearing Your Aligners

You wear each set of aligners for 1 to 2 weeks, removing them only to eat, drink anything other than water, and clean your teeth. The aligners should be worn for 20 to 22 hours per day for optimal results. Mild pressure for a day or two with each new set is normal.

5

Progress Check-ups

You visit the clinic every 6 to 8 weeks so Dr. Reeja can monitor your progress, make any necessary adjustments, and provide your next sets of aligners. These appointments are typically brief and straightforward.

6

Completion and Retainers

Once your teeth have reached their final position, you will be fitted with retainers to maintain your new smile. Retainers are essential for preventing teeth from shifting back over time. Dr. Reeja typically recommends nightly retainer wear on a long-term basis.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Invisalign treatment take in Ajman?
Treatment duration depends on the complexity of your case. Mild cases such as minor crowding or spacing typically take 6 to 9 months. Moderate cases average around 12 months, while more complex alignment issues may require 18 months or longer. During your initial consultation at Rashid Medical Centre, Dr. Reeja Moideen will assess your teeth and provide a personalised treatment timeline so you know exactly what to expect.

Flexible payment plans are also available. Book a consultation to receive a personalised quote with complete transparency and no hidden fees.
Is Invisalign better than traditional braces?
Both Invisalign and traditional braces are effective at straightening teeth, but they suit different needs. Invisalign is nearly invisible, removable for eating and cleaning, and generally more comfortable with no metal irritation. Traditional braces may be the better choice for severe or complex orthodontic cases. During your consultation, Dr. Reeja Moideen will evaluate your teeth and recommend the treatment that will deliver the best results for your specific situation.
Can adults and teenagers get Invisalign?
Absolutely. Invisalign is suitable for both adults and teenagers. There is no upper age limit, and many of our patients at Rashid Medical Centre are working professionals who appreciate the discreet appearance of clear aligners. For teenagers, Invisalign Teen includes compliance indicators and is designed to accommodate teeth that are still growing. Dr. Reeja will determine whether Invisalign is appropriate based on your individual dental assessment.
