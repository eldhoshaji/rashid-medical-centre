Our Services

Comprehensive Healthcare Services

From general medicine to advanced orthodontics, we offer a full range of dental and medical services under one roof.

Orthodontics

Expert teeth alignment with traditional braces, ceramic braces, and advanced orthodontic treatments for all ages.

Invisalign

Clear, virtually invisible aligners that straighten your teeth without the look of traditional metal braces.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Hollywood smile makeovers, veneers, teeth whitening, and bonding to give you the perfect, confident smile.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement solutions using the latest implant technology for a natural look and feel.

Root Canal Treatment

Painless root canal therapy to save damaged teeth using modern techniques and advanced anesthesia.

Children's Dentistry

Gentle and fun dental care for kids, including preventive treatments, sealants, and early orthodontic assessments.

Digital X-Rays

Low-radiation digital dental X-rays for accurate diagnosis with instant results and detailed imaging.

Teeth Cleaning

Professional dental cleaning, scaling, and polishing to maintain healthy gums and prevent tooth decay.

General Medicine

Comprehensive GP consultations for everyday health concerns, chronic disease management, and preventive care for the whole family.

Dr. Reeja Moideen

Specialist Orthodontist, MSc Orthodontics, MOrth RCSEd UK

Dr. Reeja Moideen is a specialist orthodontist with extensive knowledge and experience. She graduated from the prestigious Government Dental College Calicut, India, in 2004 and later attained post-graduation in Orthodontics from Danube University, Austria, and qualified with MOrth from the Royal College of Surgeons, Edinburgh, UK. Besides having a wealth of experience, she ensures that her patients benefit from the most up-to-date techniques in dentistry and is committed to excellence. She is highly experienced in straightening/aligning teeth, especially using the popular Invisalign treatment.

  • Correction of Multiple Teeth Alignment Issues with Fixed Metal & Ceramic Braces
  • Damon Metal & Ceramic Braces
  • Invisalign Provider / Clear Aligner Therapy
  • Jaw Expanders
  • Intraoral Scanning
  • Mini-Implants / Temporary Anchorage Devices
  • Growth Modification / Jaw Alignment Treatments
  • Habit Correction
  • Interceptive & Preventive Orthodontics: Early interventions to guide proper teeth and jaw growth
  • Surgical Orthodontics
  • Alignment of Impacted Teeth
