How to Choose the Best Dental Clinic in Ajman

Rashid Medical Centre Your Trusted Dental Partner in Ajman
26 March 2026 7 min read Ajman, UAE

Finding the right dental clinic is one of the most important healthcare decisions you can make for yourself and your family. In Ajman, where dozens of clinics compete for your attention, it can be difficult to know which one truly offers the expertise, technology, and care you deserve. This guide will help you make an informed choice.

Whether you need a routine check-up, cosmetic dentistry, orthodontic treatment, or emergency dental care, the dental clinic in Ajman you choose will directly affect the quality of your treatment and your overall experience. As someone who has practised dentistry in Ajman for over 30 years, I have seen how the right clinic can transform a patient's relationship with dental care — from something they dread to something they actually feel good about.

Below, I have outlined the eight most important factors you should evaluate when searching for the best dental clinic in Ajman. These are the same criteria I would use if I were choosing a dentist for my own family.

What to Look for in a Dental Clinic in Ajman

1

MOH Licensing and Certifications

This is non-negotiable. Every dental clinic operating in Ajman must hold a valid licence from the UAE Ministry of Health and Prevention (MOHAP). This licence confirms that the clinic meets the government's standards for hygiene, safety, equipment, and qualified personnel.

  • Ask to see the clinic's MOHAP licence — it should be displayed prominently in the reception area
  • Check that the licence is current and not expired
  • Look for additional accreditations or certifications, which indicate the clinic goes beyond the minimum standards
  • A licensed clinic is regularly inspected, meaning you can trust their sterilisation protocols and infection control measures
⚡

Our tip: If a clinic cannot show you their MOH licence when asked, that is a significant red flag. Walk away and find a clinic that is fully compliant.

2

Doctor Qualifications and Experience

The quality of your dental care depends largely on the qualifications and hands-on experience of the dentist treating you. Not all dental degrees are equal, and experience in handling complex cases matters a great deal.

  • Look for dentists with recognised degrees such as BDS, MDS, or equivalent qualifications from accredited universities
  • Check for additional specialisations or fellowships — for example, a specialist orthodontist with qualifications from the UK or Europe indicates advanced training
  • Ask how many years the dentist has been practising and whether they have experience in your specific treatment area
  • A good clinic will have multiple specialists under one roof — general dentists, orthodontists, endodontists, and oral surgeons — so you do not need to be referred elsewhere for different treatments
⚡

Our tip: Do not hesitate to ask your dentist about their qualifications and experience. A confident, skilled dentist will be happy to share this information — it builds trust and shows transparency.

3

Range of Services Offered

The best dental clinics in Ajman offer a comprehensive range of services so that your entire family's dental needs can be met in one place. This saves time, ensures continuity of care, and means your dental records are all in one system.

  • General dentistry — check-ups, cleanings, fillings, and extractions
  • Orthodontics — braces, clear aligners, and Invisalign for teeth straightening
  • Cosmetic dentistry — teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers
  • Restorative dentistry — dental implants, crowns, bridges, and dentures
  • Endodontics — root canal treatment
  • Paediatric dentistry — specialised care for children
  • Emergency dental care — same-day treatment for sudden pain, broken teeth, or trauma
4

Technology and Equipment

Modern dental technology makes treatments faster, more accurate, and more comfortable. A clinic that invests in up-to-date equipment is one that takes the quality of care seriously.

  • Digital X-rays — lower radiation exposure and instant results compared to traditional film X-rays
  • Intraoral cameras — allow you to see exactly what the dentist sees inside your mouth
  • 3D scanning and digital impressions — more accurate and comfortable than traditional moulds for crowns, aligners, and implants
  • Modern sterilisation equipment — autoclaves and proper sterilisation protocols protect you from cross-contamination
⚡

Our tip: During your first visit, take a look around. A well-maintained clinic with modern equipment reflects the level of care you can expect during treatment.

5

Patient Reviews and Ratings

In the age of Google reviews, real patient feedback is one of the most reliable ways to gauge a clinic's quality. Look beyond the star rating and read the actual comments — they reveal patterns about what the clinic does well and where it falls short.

  • Check the clinic's Google Business rating — a rating of 4.0 stars or above with a significant number of reviews is a strong indicator
  • Read reviews that mention specific treatments — this tells you about real patient experiences, not just general impressions
  • Pay attention to how the clinic responds to negative reviews — a professional, empathetic response shows they care about patient satisfaction
  • Ask friends, family, or colleagues in Ajman for personal recommendations — word of mouth remains one of the most trustworthy sources
6

Insurance Acceptance

Dental treatment can be a significant expense, so choosing a clinic that accepts your insurance plan can save you a considerable amount. The best dental clinics in Ajman work with a wide range of insurance providers to make quality care accessible.

  • Call the clinic before your visit and confirm whether your insurance plan is accepted
  • Ask about direct billing — this means the clinic settles directly with the insurance company, so you do not need to pay upfront and claim later
  • Find out what treatments are covered under your plan — some plans cover check-ups and cleanings but not cosmetic procedures
  • A clinic that partners with multiple major insurers is usually a sign of established credibility and trust in the community
7

Location and Working Hours

Convenience matters — especially for regular check-ups, ongoing orthodontic adjustments, or dental emergencies. A clinic that is easy to reach and has flexible hours makes it far more likely that you will actually keep your appointments.

  • Choose a clinic that is close to your home or workplace in Ajman — this reduces the barrier to regular visits
  • Check whether the clinic offers evening and weekend hours — many working professionals and families need appointments outside standard office hours
  • Ask about emergency availability — dental emergencies do not follow a schedule, and you need to know your clinic can see you quickly when it matters
  • Look for ample parking or easy access via public transport
8

Multilingual Staff

Ajman is home to a diverse, multicultural population. Being able to communicate comfortably with your dentist and the clinic staff — in your preferred language — makes a real difference to your experience and ensures nothing is lost in translation when discussing your treatment plan.

  • Look for clinics with staff who speak Arabic, English, Hindi, Urdu, Malayalam, and other commonly spoken languages in the UAE
  • Clear communication helps you understand your diagnosis and treatment options fully before making decisions
  • Multilingual support also makes the experience more comfortable for elderly family members or children who may not be fluent in English
⚡

Our tip: At our clinic, we see patients from over 20 different nationalities. Being able to explain a treatment plan in a patient's own language builds confidence and reduces anxiety — especially for children and first-time visitors.
